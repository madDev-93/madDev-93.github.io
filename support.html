Frequently Asked Questions

Tracking calories in Qwota is easy! You can:

  • Search our database of over 1 million foods
  • Scan barcodes using the built-in scanner (Pro)
  • Scan nutrition labels with AI recognition (Pro)
  • Take a photo of your meal for AI analysis (Pro)
  • Create custom foods and save favorites

Simply go to the Nutrition tab and tap the + button to start logging.

Qwota uses advanced formulas to calculate your personalized calorie targets:

  • BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate): Calculated using Mifflin-St Jeor or Katch-McArdle formula (if body fat % is known)
  • Activity Multiplier: Based on your activity level and exercise habits
  • 10+ Adjustment Factors: Including sleep quality, stress, dieting history, and more

You can provide additional information in Settings > Calorie Accuracy to improve your calculation accuracy.

Apple Health sync is a Pro feature. To enable it:

  1. Go to Settings > Apple Health
  2. Tap "Connect to Apple Health"
  3. Grant the requested permissions
  4. Choose what data to sync (weight, workouts, steps, etc.)

Once enabled, your data will sync automatically in the background.

To cancel your Qwota Pro subscription:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Find and tap "Qwota"
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

Your Pro access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

If you've previously purchased Qwota Pro or need to restore your subscription:

  1. Open Qwota
  2. Go to Settings > Subscription
  3. Tap "Restore Purchases"

Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

If your data isn't syncing, try these steps:

  1. Ensure you're signed in with Apple on both devices
  2. Check that iCloud is enabled in Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud
  3. Make sure Qwota is enabled under iCloud > Apps Using iCloud
  4. Connect to Wi-Fi and wait a few minutes
  5. Force close and reopen the app

If issues persist, contact us for assistance.

Troubleshooting Tips

General Issues

  • Make sure you're running the latest version of Qwota from the App Store
  • Try force-closing the app and reopening it
  • Restart your iPhone if issues persist
  • Check your internet connection for features requiring online access

Scanner Not Working

  • Ensure camera permissions are granted in Settings > Privacy > Camera
  • Clean your camera lens
  • Ensure adequate lighting when scanning
  • Hold the phone steady and wait for auto-focus

AI Coach Not Responding

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify your Pro subscription is active
  • Try signing out and back in
  • AI features may be temporarily unavailable during maintenance
